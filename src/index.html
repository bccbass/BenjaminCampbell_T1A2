Hi, I'm Ben!

I have always loved the process of building things and seeing them through from start to finish. This has manifested throughout my life in my professional career as a musician as well as my personal life. Nothing gives me greater joy than using effect pedals that I have built on a gig, or, more comically, watching my cat utilize a top entry litter box that I crafted. This same inclination exhibits itself in less materialistic ways as well. Being a musician is an extremely collaborative and social endeavor; it is a process of building structures that are more intangible. Wether it is touring with a band or playing in a Broadway pit orchestra, creating spaces that foster collaboration, good will and positive energy is imperative to building functional and fertile creative environments. In my nascent relationship with coding I have found similar parallels of feelings of accomplishment when I complete a piece of code that functions as intended or communicating with friends who are working professionally in the field; there is the possibility to build something great in the context of a group of people working together towards a common goal.
